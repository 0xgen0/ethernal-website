About the game

Explore the dungeon to discover new rooms and loot. Collaborate with others to defeat monsters, or race against them to be the first to find rare items.

Trade and sell your loot in and out of the dungeon.

But remember, if you die you will lose everything you carry for others to scavenge X

Explorer

Discover (generate) new rooms and collect elements, items and gear, But if you die, you will lose everything you carry and others can scavenge your remains.

Strategize

Manage your inventory to combat monsters, level up your character, and reach the deepest level of the dungeon.

Collect & trade

And collect elements, items and gear, But if you die, you will lose everything you carry and others can scavenge your remains.

Collaborate or race

Work with other players to defeat monsters. Or race against them to be the first to discover new rooms, collect elements and rare items.

about the technology

Built entirely on the blockchain

The dungeon is procedurally generated from the blockchain by you, the player, as you take actions. What this means is that the more active players there are, the more rooms, items, gears, monsters, and NPCs will be created in the dungeon. Being entired on the blockchain also means that we cannot change the rules or drop rates once a room is generated. Rare items stays rare. You have true ownership of your items and character – you can freely sell or trade them on marketplaces such as OpenSea.
